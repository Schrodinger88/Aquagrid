# Aquagrid Brand Guidelines

## Brand Overview

**Mission:** To restore ocean health and create sustainable coastal economies through innovative kelp farming solutions.

**Vision:** A world where ocean restoration drives economic prosperity, and where every coastal community has access to scalable, profitable marine restoration technology.

**Brand Personality:**
- **Innovative** - Cutting-edge marine technology
- **Trustworthy** - Science-backed, data-driven
- **Optimistic** - Solution-focused, forward-thinking
- **Accessible** - Clear communication, practical solutions
- **Impact-driven** - Environmental and economic results

## Logo Usage

### Primary Logo
The Aquagrid logo features a stylized kelp frond with flowing water elements, representing the intersection of marine biology and sustainable technology.

**Logo Components:**
- Kelp frond (dark green) - represents marine restoration
- Water flow (blue gradient) - represents ocean health and movement
- Triangle base (navy) - represents stability and foundation
- "AQUA GRID" wordmark (gray) - modern, professional typography

### Logo Variations
1. **Full Logo with Tagline** - Use for main headers, landing pages, official documents
2. **Logo Only** - Use for favicon, social media profile pictures, small applications

### Clear Space
Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "A" in "AQUA" on all sides.

### Minimum Size
- Digital: 120px width minimum
- Print: 1 inch width minimum

### Logo Don'ts
- Don't distort or stretch the logo
- Don't change the colors
- Don't rotate the logo
- Don't add effects (shadows, gradients, outlines)
- Don't place on busy backgrounds without proper contrast

## Typography

### Primary Font: TeX Gyre Adventor Bold
- **Usage:** Headings (H1, H2, H3), Logo, Navigation, Buttons
- **Character:** Modern, bold, confident
- **Weights:** Bold (700)
- **Fallback:** Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif

**Heading Sizes:**
- H1: 48px / 3rem (Desktop), 36px / 2.25rem (Mobile)
- H2: 36px / 2.25rem (Desktop), 28px / 1.75rem (Mobile)
- H3: 28px / 1.75rem (Desktop), 24px / 1.5rem (Mobile)
- H4: 24px / 1.5rem (Desktop), 20px / 1.25rem (Mobile)

### Secondary Font: Flatory Sans SemiCondensed
- **Usage:** Body text, paragraphs, descriptions, captions
- **Character:** Clean, readable, efficient
- **Weights:** Regular (400), Medium (500)
- **Fallback:** "Helvetica Neue", Arial, sans-serif

**Body Text Sizes:**
- Body Large: 18px / 1.125rem
- Body Regular: 16px / 1rem
- Body Small: 14px / 0.875rem
- Caption: 12px / 0.75rem

### Typography Guidelines
- **Line Height:** 1.6 for body text, 1.3 for headings
- **Letter Spacing:** Normal for body, slightly expanded (+0.5px) for ALL CAPS
- **Text Color:** #333333 for body text on light backgrounds
- **Link Color:** #003273 (Deep Ocean Blue), underline on hover

## Brand Messaging

### Primary Tagline
**"Green Solutions for Blue Oceans"**

### Alternative Taglines (Contextual Use)
- "Where kelp meets clean waters" - Environmental focus
- "Leading the wave in ocean restoration" - Innovation focus
- "Protecting oceans with smart solutions" - Technology focus
- "Turning the tide with Aquagrid" - Action-oriented
- "Creating ripples of change" - Impact focus

### Voice & Tone

**Brand Voice:** Professional yet approachable, scientifically grounded but not academic, optimistic without greenwashing

**Writing Principles:**
1. **Be Clear:** Avoid jargon; explain complex concepts simply
2. **Be Confident:** We have proven solutions backed by science
3. **Be Honest:** Share real data, acknowledge challenges
4. **Be Inspiring:** Focus on impact and positive change
5. **Be Practical:** Emphasize actionable solutions and concrete results

**Tone Variations by Context:**
- **Website Homepage:** Inspiring, bold, visionary
- **Technical Documentation:** Precise, detailed, professional
- **Investor Materials:** Data-driven, confident, ROI-focused
- **Social Media:** Engaging, educational, optimistic
- **Customer Support:** Helpful, patient, solution-oriented

### Key Messages

**Environmental Message:**
"Ocean acidification threatens marine ecosystems worldwide. Aquagrid's kelp farming technology actively restores ocean chemistry while creating thriving marine habitats."

**Economic Message:**
"Sustainable ocean restoration doesn't have to sacrifice profitability. Our systems deliver measurable environmental impact alongside strong financial returns."

**Innovation Message:**
"We've engineered nature's most efficient carbon capture organism into a scalable, modular farming system that works with ocean ecosystems, not against them."

**Social Impact Message:**
"Every Aquagrid installation creates jobs, strengthens coastal economies, and gives communities a stake in ocean health."

## Target Audience Profiles

### Primary Audience: Environmental Decision-Makers
**Age:** 35-55  
**Background:** College-educated, often in environmental science, marine biology, sustainability  
**Values:** Evidence-based solutions, measurable impact, long-term thinking  
**Pain Points:** Limited budgets, pressure for results, need for scalable solutions  
**Message Focus:** ROI, environmental metrics, proven technology

### Secondary Audience: Fishery Operators
**Age:** 40-60  
**Background:** Practical, business-oriented, deeply connected to marine resources  
**Values:** Sustainability that supports livelihood, community, tradition  
**Pain Points:** Declining catches, regulatory pressure, economic uncertainty  
**Message Focus:** Fish population recovery, diversified income, practical implementation

### Tertiary Audience: Policy Makers
**Age:** 45-65  
**Background:** Government agencies, environmental regulation, coastal management  
**Values:** Public good, regulatory compliance, constituent support  
**Pain Points:** Climate commitments, budget constraints, political pressures  
**Message Focus:** Climate action, job creation, community benefit

## Visual Style

### Photography Style
**Subject Matter:**
- Kelp forests (underwater and surface)
- Coastal landscapes
- Marine life diversity
- Installation process
- Team members working
- Community impact

**Style Guidelines:**
- Natural lighting preferred
- Vibrant but realistic colors
- Focus on action and impact
- Include people when possible (builds connection)
- Show scale and scope
- Avoid overly staged or stock-looking images

**Color Treatment:**
- Enhance blues and greens naturally
- Maintain realistic ocean colors
- Avoid heavy filtering or artificial looks

### Iconography
**Style:** Clean, modern line icons with 2px stroke weight

**Subject Matter:**
- Marine elements (waves, kelp, fish)
- Environmental metrics (CO2, pH, growth)
- Technology (monitoring, data, systems)
- Economic (ROI, revenue, growth)

**Colors:** Use brand colors, primarily Deep Ocean Blue (#003273) on light backgrounds

### Illustrations
**Style:** Semi-flat design with subtle depth

**Use Cases:**
- Explaining complex processes
- Showing system components
- Visualizing data and impact
- Educational content

**Color Palette:** Use full brand palette, emphasize greens and blues

### Data Visualization
**Chart Types:**
- Line graphs for growth and trends
- Bar charts for comparisons
- Pie charts for composition (use sparingly)
- Maps for geographic data
- Infographics for impact metrics

**Colors:** Use Data Blue (#3498DB) as primary, accent with brand colors

## UI/UX Guidelines

### Button Styles

**Primary Button:**
- Background: #125A1A (Ocean Green)
- Text: White, TeX Gyre Adventor Bold
- Padding: 12px 32px
- Border Radius: 4px
- Hover: Darken to #0D4013

**Secondary Button:**
- Background: Transparent
- Border: 2px solid #003273 (Deep Ocean Blue)
- Text: #003273, TeX Gyre Adventor Bold
- Padding: 10px 30px
- Hover: Background #003273, Text White

**CTA Button (High Priority):**
- Background: #003273 (Deep Ocean Blue)
- Text: White, TeX Gyre Adventor Bold
- Padding: 14px 36px
- Border Radius: 6px
- Hover: Darken to #002050, add slight shadow

### Cards & Containers
- Background: White or #F5F5F5
- Border: None or 1px solid #E0E0E0
- Border Radius: 8px
- Shadow: 0 2px 8px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.08)
- Padding: 24px

### Spacing System
Use 8px base unit:
- xs: 8px
- sm: 16px
- md: 24px
- lg: 32px
- xl: 48px
- 2xl: 64px
- 3xl: 96px

### Responsive Breakpoints
- Mobile: 320px - 767px
- Tablet: 768px - 1023px
- Desktop: 1024px - 1439px
- Large Desktop: 1440px+

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do:
✅ Use ocean and marine imagery authentically  
✅ Show real data and research  
✅ Highlight both environmental and economic benefits  
✅ Feature real team members and partners  
✅ Maintain professional credibility  
✅ Be transparent about challenges and progress  
✅ Use accessible language  

### Don't:
❌ Greenwash or exaggerate environmental claims  
❌ Use generic stock photos of oceans  
❌ Ignore the economic viability aspect  
❌ Over-promise on timelines or results  
❌ Use overly technical jargon without explanation  
❌ Neglect accessibility in design  
❌ Copy competitor messaging or visuals
