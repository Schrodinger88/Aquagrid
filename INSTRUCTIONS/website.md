# Aqua Grid Website Content

Based on the official Pitch Deck, Marketing Package, and Technical Research from the Drive Download.

## Hero Section
**Slogan:** Green Solutions for Blue Oceans
**Mission:** Modular kelp farming technology that restores marine ecosystems and generates sustainable revenue for coastal communities.

## The Crisis
- Ocean acidification has increased 30% since the Industrial Revolution.
- Projected 150% rise by the end of the century.
- CO₂ absorption increases water pH, threatening shell-building marine life and directly impacting Canada's multi-million dollar shellfish industry.

## Our Solution: Modular Kelp Farming
- System combines marine engineering with kelp (nature's most powerful carbon capture organism).
- **Design:** Standard unit is 200ft × 10ft. Metal plate frame held by buoys with vertical kelp growth lines. Modular and customizable.
- **Key Innovation:** Siphon Pump System increases nutrient availability for kelp year-round, boosting growth.
- **Farmer-First:** Designed to integrate easily into existing fisheries without interrupting operations. Requires minimal manpower for annual harvest.

## Environmental & Economic Impact (Per Unit, Annually)
- **Carbon Capture:** 1,130 kg CO₂ removed per year.
- **Oxygen Production:** 1,100 kg O₂ produced per year. (Produces up to 100x more oxygen than land forests of the same size).
- **Setup Cost:** $6,500.
- **Maintenance:** $600/year.
- **Annual Revenue:** $3,000+ per year (from kelp sales + increased shellfish farm yield).
- **Payback Period:** 3 Years to break-even.
- **Gross Margin:** 20% on system sales.

## Market Opportunity
- Global Seaweed Market (2024): $10.47B
- Projected Market (2032): $26.61B
- CAGR (2025–2032): 12.36%
- US Seaweed Market: $1.46B
- Seaweed Cosmetics Market: Growing from $2.1B to $3.4B.
- Global Ecosystem Services Value: ~$500B annually (Fisheries: $29,900/ha/year, Nutrient Cycling: $73,800/ha/year).

## Kelp Applications (After Harvest)
- Organic Fertilizer (Nutrient-rich soil conditioner)
- Bioenergy Production (Biogas, bioethanol)
- Bioplastics & Packaging (From alginates)
- Biochar Production (Locks carbon/pollutants)
- Heavy Metal Recovery
- Pharmaceutical & Cosmetics (Anti-aging, hydration)
