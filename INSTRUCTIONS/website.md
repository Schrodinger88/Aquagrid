## The Crisis We're Solving

Our oceans are in crisis. Ocean acidification has increased by 30% since the Industrial Revolution, threatening marine life and the livelihoods of millions who depend on healthy fisheries.

### The Problems

**Ocean Acidification**  
Rising CO2 levels are making our oceans more acidic, dissolving the shells and skeletons of marine organisms. Coral reefs, shellfish, and entire food chains are collapsing under the chemical stress.

**Collapsing Fisheries**  
Global fish stocks have declined by 90% since 1950. Overfishing combined with habitat degradation has pushed marine ecosystems to the brink, threatening food security for billions.

**Inadequate Solutions**  
Current approaches are either too expensive, too slow, or too localized to make a meaningful impact. We need scalable, economically viable solutions that work with nature, not against it.

### Why Aquagrid?

Aquagrid transforms the problem into an opportunity. Our modular kelp farming systems don't just capture carbon—they actively restore ocean chemistry, create thriving marine habitats, and generate sustainable income through kelp harvests that benefit agriculture and industry.

## How It Works

### Phase 1: Design & Planning (Months 1-2)
Site assessment and environmental analysis. We work with you to identify optimal locations, obtain necessary permits, and design a system tailored to your coastal conditions.

### Phase 2: Installation (Months 3-4)
Rapid deployment of modular units. Our team handles all installation with minimal disruption to existing marine activities. Systems are operational within weeks of deployment.

### Phase 3: Growth & Monitoring (Months 5-8)
Kelp grows rapidly—up to 2 feet per day in optimal conditions. Our smart monitoring system tracks growth, water quality, and ecosystem health in real-time via cloud dashboard.

First harvest yields immediate returns. Continuous growth cycles provide multiple harvests per year while environmental benefits compound over time.

## FAQ

**How much space do I need?**  
Our modular systems scale from 0.5 hectares to 100+ hectares. We can design solutions for nearly any coastal site with appropriate depth and current conditions.

**What are the permit requirements?**  
Requirements vary by location. We assist with all permitting processes, including environmental assessments, coastal zone management approvals, and marine space planning.

**How is kelp harvested and sold?**  
We provide harvesting equipment and training. We also connect you with our network of kelp buyers, or you can sell directly to local agricultural and industrial customers.

**What happens in storms?**  
Our systems are engineered for extreme weather. Modular units can be temporarily submerged or relocated. We've tested designs in conditions up to Category 3 hurricane-force winds.

**Is this profitable?**  
Yes. After initial investment recovery (typically 2.5 years), operations generate $30,000-$40,000 profit per hectare annually, with potential for higher returns as kelp markets expand.

**What if I'm not coastal?**  
We're developing partnerships with coastal operators. You can invest in Aquagrid installations and receive a share of kelp harvest revenues while supporting ocean restoration.
